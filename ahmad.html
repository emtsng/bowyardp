Ahmad Umar Hassan is a Partner at Bowyard Partners, with a practice that spans corporate transactions, energy advisory, regulatory engagements and financial services law. He brings a transactional mindset and a deep understanding of the regulatory landscape to every client matter.

Ahmad's transactional practice covers corporate acquisitions, joint ventures, energy concessions, infrastructure project financing, and regulatory advisory on matters with both domestic and cross-border dimensions. He advises clients across the public and private sectors, with a particular focus on energy, financial services and government-linked transactions.

He is known among clients for his clarity of thinking, his ability to move matters forward under pressure, and for providing advice that is both legally sound and commercially useful — grounding complex legal analysis in the practical realities of the client's business and sector.

Ahmad's approach to practice is rooted in a belief that a good lawyer must first be a good adviser — someone who understands the client's world well enough to see where the real risks and opportunities lie, and who can structure legal solutions around that understanding.
