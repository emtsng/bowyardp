What We Do

Practices &
Industries.

Five core legal practices. Seven industry specialisations. Built around the sectors and transaction types where our clients operate — and where we go deep.

Practices. Industries. Focus.
We organise our work into two categories: Practices — the legal disciplines we deploy — and Industries — the sectors where we have built deep, accumulated expertise. This structure reflects how we actually work: with the commercial knowledge of an industry insider and the legal precision of a specialist. Each practice and industry group is led at partner level.
Legal Capabilities

Practices

The legal disciplines that form the core of our advisory work — each led by a partner with deep expertise in the relevant area of law and a track record of delivery at the highest level.

01
Corporate & Commercial

We advise clients on structuring transactions, negotiating agreements and managing corporate governance matters — covering M&A, joint ventures, company secretarial, due diligence, commercial contracts and corporate restructuring.

→
02
Governance, Compliance & Policy

We advise clients on ethical governance frameworks, regulatory compliance and policies aligned with local and international standards — including AML/CFT advisory, ESG governance, corporate audits and public procurement compliance.

→
03
Disputes Resolution & Crisis Management

We represent clients in commercial litigation, international arbitration and regulatory proceedings — including enforcement of judgments, emergency injunctions and pre-dispute risk advisory to prevent matters from escalating.

→
04
Business Advisory

We support clients with strategic planning, operational risk management, corporate sustainability and commercial structuring for growth — including feasibility studies, tax structuring, business intelligence and PPP advisory.

→
05
Private Clients & Trusts

We provide discreet advisory to high-net-worth individuals and family offices on estate planning, succession, trust structures, asset protection and intergenerational wealth arrangements.

→
Sector Focus

Industries

The sectors where our team has built accumulated expertise — enabling us to advise on the commercial, regulatory and operational dimensions that only come from working deeply inside an industry over time.

01
Energy & Natural Resources

Specialist upstream and midstream advisory on licences, concession agreements, farm-in/farm-out transactions, FPSO contracts and regulatory compliance under the Petroleum Industry Act.

→
02
Infrastructure, Power & PPP

We advise sponsors, developers, contractors and public institutions on the legal and commercial structuring of infrastructure projects — including power generation, PPP concessions, EPC contracts, project finance and government interface.

→
03
Construction & Real Estate

We advise developers, contractors and investors on construction contracts, development transactions, regulatory approvals, property acquisitions, joint ventures and land titling across Nigeria.

→
04
Financial Services & Banking

We advise banks, financial institutions, fintechs and borrowers on lending transactions, security structuring, regulatory compliance, CBN and SEC filings, and capital markets documentation.

→
05
Transport, Maritime & Logistics

We advise clients in the maritime, aviation and logistics sectors on regulatory compliance, ship registration, port operations, carriage of goods disputes and cross-border transport arrangements.

→
06
Technology & Innovation

We advise technology companies, start-ups and investors on IP protection, data privacy, technology contracts, e-commerce regulation and venture capital transactions.

→
07
Government & Public Sector

We advise government agencies, parastatals and public institutions on legal frameworks, procurement processes, regulatory reforms, inter-governmental agreements and public law matters.
