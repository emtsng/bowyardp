The Firm

Who We Are

Bowyard Partners is a commercially focused law firm providing legal advisory, transaction support, regulatory compliance and dispute resolution to clients operating in complex and high-value sectors.

We combine legal knowledge with sector expertise, commercial awareness and a disciplined understanding of the markets in which our clients operate. Our work is built around strong sector focus in corporate and commercial practice, energy and infrastructure, disputes resolution, project finance, financial services, real estate and construction.

Bowyard Partners renders a careful combination of legal advisory, consulting capability and business value — advising clients where legal outcomes must be aligned with commercial objectives.

Our Philosophy

Partner-Led. Commercially Aware.

Client instructions at Bowyard Partners receive senior-level attention from instruction to close-out. We are not a volume firm. We are selective in the matters we take on — so that the clients we represent receive quality, responsiveness and strategic alignment at every stage.

We believe the best legal work happens when lawyers understand the business, not just the law. Every matter is handled with that conviction.
