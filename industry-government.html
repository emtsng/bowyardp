Government
& Public Sector.

Where law serves the public interest. We advise federal and state government agencies, parastatals and government-linked entities on the legal, regulatory and commercial dimensions of public sector operations and transactions.

Government clients require counsel that understands both the law and the public interest it serves.
Our Government & Public Sector practice advises federal and state ministries, departments and agencies, government corporations, parastatals and development institutions on the full range of legal and regulatory matters affecting their mandates. We understand the legal frameworks governing public sector operations in Nigeria — including the Public Procurement Act, CAMA 2020 (as it applies to government bodies), constitutional frameworks, public finance law and the regulatory obligations applicable to public institutions. We advise with the rigour and discretion that government mandates demand.
What We Do

Our Capabilities

We advise public institutions on the legal dimensions of their mandates — from procurement and regulatory reform to commercial transactions and inter-governmental arrangements.

01

Legal Framework Development

We advise government agencies on the development of legal frameworks — including sector-specific legislation, subsidiary legislation, regulatory guidelines and administrative instruments. We assist with the legal design of institutions, mandates and governance structures.

02

Public Procurement Advisory

We advise government agencies on compliance with the Public Procurement Act — including bid documentation, procurement planning, due process compliance, contract award and the legal dimensions of procurement disputes and bid challenges. We also advise private sector suppliers participating in public procurement processes.

03

Regulatory Reform Engagements

We advise on regulatory reform — including the review and update of sector regulatory frameworks, the establishment of new regulatory bodies and the engagement with policy reform processes across government. We help agencies modernise their regulatory frameworks and build effective enforcement capacity.

04

Inter-Governmental Agreements

We advise on inter-governmental agreements — including MOUs between federal and state governments, bilateral arrangements between Nigerian agencies and international bodies, and the legal framework governing co-operation between government entities at different tiers of the Nigerian federation.

05

Public Law & Administrative Law

We advise government bodies on public law matters — including the legality of administrative decisions, judicial review risk, constitutional boundaries of government authority, freedom of information compliance and the legal dimensions of enforcement decisions and regulatory proceedings.

06

Policy Advisory & Legislative Drafting

We advise on policy development and assist in the drafting of legislation, subsidiary legislation and regulatory instruments. We work with government legal teams, national assembly committees and state executive councils on the legal design and drafting of policy initiatives.

07

MDA Compliance & Audit

We advise MDAs on their compliance obligations — including statutory reporting, public accounts compliance, internal control frameworks, anti-corruption obligations and the legal dimensions of audit findings and accountability mechanisms under the Financial Regulations and other applicable frameworks.

08

Government Commercial Transactions

We advise government agencies on commercial transactions — including contracts with private sector counterparties, service level agreements, concession agreements, licensing arrangements and the structuring of government commercial activities in compliance with applicable public law constraints.
