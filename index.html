Service We Provide

Corporate & Commercial

Our corporate and commercial practice provides comprehensive on incorporation, contracts, restructuring, M&A, etc. We also support start-up and matured companies and businesses across a broad spectrum of their operations.

Energy & Infrastructure

Our vibrant energy practice comprises a team of specialist attorneys who advise energy project developers, operators, financiers, and regulators on the entire lifecycle of energy projects across different fuel sources. Our expertise cut across legal support across power projects, oil & gas, PPPs, project structuring, and ESG frameworks.

Transportation & Maritime

We advise on transportation infrastructure, ranging from roads, bridges, airports, and ports, including regulatory compliance and project development, cargo transport, maritime operations, etc.

Disputes Resolution

We provide comprehensive disputes services, from prevention to strategic management, and resolution through arbitration, mediation and litigation to resolve commercial disputes.
