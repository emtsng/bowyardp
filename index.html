Our Identity

The bow leads. The yard builds.

The name Bowyard captures our operating idea. The bow is the forward-most part of a vessel — a symbol of direction, navigation and courage in uncertain waters. The yard is a place where things are built, assembled and launched — a symbol of enterprise and creation.

Together: leading clients forward while helping them build, structure and launch ambitious ventures.
