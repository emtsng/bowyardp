Our Journey

A Firm Built
With Purpose.

From a focused boutique practice to a leading corporate law firm — the story of how Bowyard Partners was built, and where we are headed.

Direction.
Structure.
Creation.
Bowyard Partners was built on a simple conviction: that Nigerian businesses operating in complex sectors deserved a law firm with the sophistication, sector depth and commercial orientation to match their ambitions. From our earliest instructions, we have built our practice around the belief that legal excellence and commercial value are not in tension — they are the same thing.
Milestones

The Bowyard Story

Over a decade of building a practice that leads clients through complex, high-value work.

About the Firm
2012
The Foundation

Bowyard Partners is established in Abuja by Atiku Jafar with a clear mandate: to provide commercially oriented legal advisory to businesses operating in Nigeria's most complex and high-value sectors. The name — Bowyard — captures the firm's operating philosophy from the outset.

2014
First Major Mandates

The firm secures its first significant corporate and energy mandates, advising on transactions across the power and infrastructure sectors. The practice quickly develops a reputation for partner-led service and sector-specific depth.

2016
Lagos Office Opened

Responding to growing client demand from Nigeria's commercial capital, Bowyard Partners opens its Lagos office. This dual-city presence enables the firm to serve clients across Nigeria's two most active business hubs — Abuja and Lagos.

2018
Energy & Infrastructure Practice

A dedicated energy and infrastructure practice is established, reflecting the firm's growing instruction base in oil & gas, renewables and public-private partnership projects. Ahmad Muhibbah joins as Managing Partner, strengthening the firm's corporate and commercial capability.

2020
Navigating Complexity

During a period of significant regulatory and economic complexity, Bowyard Partners advises clients across sectors on business continuity, restructuring, regulatory compliance and crisis management. The firm's commercial orientation proves decisive for clients navigating uncertain conditions.

2022
Governance & Compliance Practice

A formal Governance, Compliance & Policy practice is launched, advising clients on AML/CFT compliance, ESG governance, public procurement and regulatory frameworks. The firm is recognised for its cross-sector regulatory expertise.

2024
Today — 13 Practice Areas

Bowyard Partners today operates across 13 practice areas with a team of partners, senior associates and professionals delivering for clients in corporate, energy, infrastructure, financial services, real estate, disputes and government sectors. The firm remains partner-led, commercially oriented and built for ambition.

The Name

Why Bowyard?

The bow is the forward-most point of a vessel — the part that cuts through water first. It symbolises direction, navigation and the courage to move forward in uncertain conditions.

The yard is a place where vessels are built, maintained and launched. It symbolises structure, craft, and the steady work of creating something that endures.

Together, Bowyard captures exactly what this firm does: leading clients forward while helping them build, structure and launch ambitious ventures.
