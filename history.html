Our Journey

A Firm Built
With Purpose.

From a boutique practice to a growing full-service law firm — the story of how Bowyard Partners was built, and where we are headed.

Direction.
Structure.
Creation.
Bowyard Partners was founded on the belief that legal advice should do more than interpret the law - it should shape strategy, facilitate investment, and create sustained commercial value. From our earliest engagements, we have built a multidisciplinary practice centred on sectors driving economic development, particularly infrastructure, energy, finance, construction, real estate, and public sector reform. Today, we advise diverse clients on complex transactions and disputes. Our growth has been deliberately guided by industry knowledge, commercial awareness and an unwavering commitment to client success.
Milestones

The Bowyard Story

Every milestone in our journey reflects a deliberate investment in capability and long-term client relationships. As our clients have grown, so have we continuously expanded and equipped our team to meet growing legal and commercial challenges.

About the Firm
2021
The Beginning

Bowyard Partners is established with a clear vision: to build a modern commercial law firm that combines technical excellence with practical business judgment. From inception, the firm focuses on delivering commercially driven legal solutions for businesses operating in regulated and capital-intensive industries. The name - Bowyard - captures the firm's operating philosophy from the outset.

2022
Building a Foundation

The firm begins advising clients across corporate transactions, regulatory compliance, project development and commercial contracting, laying the foundations for long-term relationships across the private and public sectors.

2023
Energy & Infrastructure Expansion

Bowyard Partners expands its infrastructure and energy capabilities, advising on major projects involving ports, transportation, environmental remediation, power, renewable energy and public-private partnerships. These engagements establish the firm's reputation within Nigeria's infrastructure space.

2024
Complex Projects. Trusted Counsel.

The firm's growing portfolio includes advisory work on concession structures, engineering and construction projects, joint ventures, project finance, shipbuilding initiatives and large-scale commercial transactions. Alongside this growth, Bowyard strengthens its dispute resolution, corporate governance and regulatory advisory practices.

Today
Looking Ahead

Today, Bowyard Partners advises corporations, financial institutions, investors, government entities and development partners across diverse sectors. As we continue to grow, our ambition remains unchanged: to become one of Africa's foremost commercial law firms, recognised for shaping transformative projects, delivering exceptional client service and contributing meaningfully to economic development.

The Name

Why Bowyard?

The bow is the forward-most point of a vessel — the part that cuts through water first. It symbolises direction, navigation and the courage to move forward in uncertain conditions.

The yard is a place where vessels are built, maintained and launched. It symbolises structure, craft, and the steady work of creating something that endures.

Together, Bowyard captures exactly what this firm does: leading clients forward while helping them build, structure and launch ambitious ventures.
