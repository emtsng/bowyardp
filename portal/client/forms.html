Corporate Affairs Commission — most common client filing instructions
Form CAC 1.1
Company Registration
Application for Registration of a new company with the Corporate Affairs Commission pursuant to CAMA 2020.
  • Company name & type (Ltd, Guarantee, Unlimited)
  • Registered office address
  • Authorised share capital details
  • First directors — names, IDs, addresses
  • Company secretary details

Form CAC 7
Appointment of Director
Notify the CAC of the appointment of a new director pursuant to Section 321 of CAMA 2020.
  • Company registration number
  • New director's full particulars
  • Valid government-issued ID
  • Service and residential addresses
  • Date of appointment

Form CAC 2A
Return of Allotment
File a return of allotment of shares (post-incorporation) pursuant to Section 129 of CAMA 2020.
  • Company number & date of resolution
  • Number and type of shares allotted
  • Cash and non-cash consideration details
  • Details of each allottee (up to 6)
  • Post-allotment shareholder list
