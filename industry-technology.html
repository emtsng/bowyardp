Industry

Technology
& Innovation.

The law behind the future. We advise technology companies, fintechs, digital platforms and innovation-driven businesses on the legal framework governing Nigeria's rapidly evolving technology sector.

Technology moves fast. The law moves to catch up. We help clients stay ahead of both.
Our Technology & Innovation practice advises startups, fintechs, digital platforms, technology companies and investors on the full range of legal issues affecting technology businesses in Nigeria. From intellectual property protection and data privacy compliance to fintech licensing, venture capital transactions and regulatory engagement with the NCC and CBN, we provide practical, expert legal guidance to technology-driven clients who need counsel that understands both the law and the industry.
What We Do

Our Capabilities

We advise across the legal landscape of Nigeria's technology sector — from IP and data protection to fintech regulation, digital contracts and startup transactions.

01

Intellectual Property Protection

We advise technology companies on the protection of intellectual property — including trade mark registration, copyright protection, trade secret policies, technology licensing and IP assignment in the context of employment, founder arrangements and investment transactions.

02

Data Protection & NDPA Compliance

We advise technology companies on compliance with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) — including data protection impact assessments, privacy policy design, data processing agreements, cross-border data transfers and engagement with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC).

03

Technology & SaaS Contracts

We draft and negotiate technology contracts — including software development agreements, SaaS terms, API agreements, cloud service arrangements, technology transfer agreements and IT outsourcing contracts. We protect our clients' positions while enabling commercial partnerships to work.

04

E-Commerce & Platform Regulation

We advise e-commerce operators and digital platform businesses on the legal framework governing online trade and digital services — including FCCPC Consumer Protection Regulations, the Nigeria Electronic Commerce Policy, terms and conditions frameworks and digital marketing regulation.

05

Venture Capital & Startup Transactions

We advise founders, investors and accelerators on startup and venture capital transactions — including equity investment documentation (SAFEs, convertible notes, priced rounds), founder arrangements, cap table structuring, corporate governance and due diligence on technology assets.

06

Digital Payments Regulation

We advise digital payment companies on the regulatory framework governing payment systems in Nigeria — including CBN payment service bank licensing, mobile money operator requirements, NIBSS interface arrangements, PCI-DSS compliance considerations and the regulatory framework for digital wallets and stored value.

07

Cybersecurity & Incident Response

We advise organisations on the legal dimensions of cybersecurity — including cybersecurity policy development, breach notification obligations under the NDPA and NITDA frameworks, legal liability for data breaches and legal support in managing the regulatory and litigation dimensions of cyber incidents.

08

Regulatory Engagement — NCC, CBN & Others

We advise technology companies on engagement with sector regulators — including the Nigerian Communications Commission (NCC), the CBN, NITDA and FCCPC. We assist with licence applications, regulatory submissions, consultation responses and the management of regulatory relationships.
