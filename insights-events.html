June 2025

Youth Energy Summit (YES! 2025) — "Youth Innovation in Future Energy Technologies" Panel
CTICC 2, Cape Town, South Africa Conference / Panel
Managing Partner Atiku Jafar joined a panel on youth innovation in future energy technologies at YES! 2025, organised by EnergyNet Ltd. and the Africa Energy Forum. Drawing on his background in Karim Lamido — a Nigerian community of over 200,000 people with no grid electricity — he addressed the legal scaffolding needed to unlock youth-led clean energy solutions across sub-Saharan Africa, including enabling licensing regimes, energy sandboxes, model PPAs and de-risking tools for early-stage ventures in the clean energy sector.

Africa Summit
Khalifa Musa and Atiku Jafar attended the Africa Gas Innovation Summit (AGIS) 2024, themed "Igniting the Future: Driving Sustainability in Africa's Energy Landscape through Gas Technology and Innovation." Africa Natural Resources and Energy Investment Summit (AFNIS) 2024
Africa Summit
Khalifa Musa and Atiku Jafar attended the Africa Natural Resources and Energy Investment Summit (AFNIS) 2024, themed "Natural Resources for Economic Development." The summit convened key stakeholders across the power, mining, oil & gas, agriculture and finance sectors to explore investment opportunities, facilitate cross-sectoral collaboration, and discuss sustainable development strategies. Building on prior discussions around energy access and the imperative of a just and equitable energy transition, the 2024 edition underscored the pivotal role of Africa's natural resources in fuelling economic prosperity and empowering marginalised communities.

95th Power Dialogue — The Electricity Act 2023: One Year After
The Ventures Park, Maitama, Abuja Panel  ·  Dialogue
Atiku Jafar joined the 95th Power Dialogue as a panelist, reviewing progress made since the enactment of the Electricity Act 2023 and examining the government's past and future initiatives to create an enabling environment for the private sector, investors, development partners and states to leverage the Act's provisions. Kohn & Co.) and Damilola Alada (Executive Associate, Bloomfield LP). The dialogue was held at The Ventures Park, 5 Kwaji Close, Maitama, Abuja.

October 2022
31 Oct – 1 Nov
ECOWAS Sustainable Energy Forum (ESEF) 2022
ECREEE  ·  West Africa Forum
Bowyard Partners attended West Africa's premier sustainable energy event — the ECOWAS Sustainable Energy Forum (ESEF) 2022, organised by the ECOWAS Centre for Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency (ECREEE). The forum brought together policy makers, regional and international private sector actors, and financial institutions to discuss and improve the policy and regulatory landscapes for private sector investment in sustainable energy across ECOWAS member states. The message was clear: catalysing investment in sustainable energy must be anchored by strong policy initiatives and cross-sector partnerships.
