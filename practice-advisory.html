Practice

Business
Advisory.

Strategic clarity for complex commercial decisions. We advise ambitious businesses on the commercial, financial and strategic dimensions of their most important decisions.

Good legal advice begins with understanding the business.
Our Business Advisory practice sits at the intersection of law, commerce and strategy. We work with clients — from growth-stage businesses to established enterprises and government-linked entities — who need more than legal advice. They need a partner who understands their business, their sector and the strategic environment in which they operate. We provide that counsel: integrated, commercially grounded and focused on outcomes that create lasting value.
What We Do

Our Capabilities

We advise on strategy, commercial structuring, risk and business development — integrating legal and commercial thinking to help clients make better decisions.

01

Strategic Business Planning

We work with boards and management teams on strategic planning exercises — helping define objectives, assess options and identify the legal, regulatory and commercial implications of strategic choices. We translate strategy into implementable plans.

02

Commercial Structuring & Optimisation

We advise on the commercial and legal structuring of business operations — including group structures, operating model design, joint venture arrangements and commercial arrangements with partners, distributors and government entities.

03

Feasibility Studies & Business Cases

We support clients in developing feasibility assessments and business cases for new ventures, market entries, infrastructure projects and investment proposals — incorporating legal, regulatory, commercial and operational dimensions.

04

Tax Planning & Structuring

We advise on the tax implications of business structures, transactions and commercial arrangements — helping clients implement tax-efficient structures that are compliant with Nigerian tax law and aligned with FIRS and state revenue authority requirements.

05

Risk Assessment & Management

We conduct legal risk assessments for businesses entering new markets, launching new products, pursuing acquisitions or entering into significant commercial arrangements — identifying exposure, advising on mitigation and helping management make informed risk decisions.

06

Market Entry Advisory

We advise international businesses entering the Nigerian market on the legal, regulatory and commercial requirements for establishing operations — including entity selection, registration, licensing, sector regulatory approvals and initial commercial arrangements.

07

PPP & Government Interface Advisory

We advise private sector participants on engaging with government — including structuring PPP proposals, understanding public procurement frameworks, navigating inter-agency approvals and managing the commercial and legal dimensions of government partnerships.

08

Corporate Sustainability

We advise businesses on developing corporate sustainability strategies that align with their commercial objectives, investor expectations and regulatory environment — including ESG integration, sustainability reporting and long-term value creation frameworks.
