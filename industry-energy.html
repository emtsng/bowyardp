Energy &
Natural Resources.

The sector that powers Nigeria's economy. We advise operators, investors and government agencies on every dimension of the Nigerian energy landscape — from upstream transactions to the energy transition.

Nigeria's energy sector demands legal counsel that understands both the law and the industry.
Our Energy & Natural Resources practice is one of our most established capabilities. We advise international oil companies, indigenous operators, investors, financial institutions and government agencies on the full range of legal issues affecting the Nigerian energy sector — from licensing and upstream transactions to gas commercialisation, renewables and the complex regulatory framework under the Petroleum Industry Act (PIA). We understand the commercial realities of the sector and the regulatory environment in which our clients operate.
What We Do

Our Capabilities

From upstream exploration to downstream distribution, from licensing to disputes, we provide sector-specific legal counsel across the entire energy value chain.

01

Upstream E&P Advisory

We advise on exploration and production activities — including OML/OPL transactions, asset acquisitions, divestments, participation agreements and the legal dimensions of field development. We understand the commercial dynamics of Nigerian upstream and provide advice that reflects the real world.

02

PIA Compliance & Regulatory Advisory

We advise clients on compliance with the Petroleum Industry Act — including regulatory engagement with NUPRC and NMDPRA, licence conversion, host community obligations and the transition obligations imposed on existing operators under the new legal framework.

03

Licence Applications & Management

We advise on licence applications, licence extensions, renewals and the management of licence conditions — including co-ordinating engagement with NUPRC, NMDPRA, the Ministry of Petroleum Resources and other relevant agencies.

04

Farm-in / Farm-out Transactions

We advise operators and investors on farm-in and farm-out transactions — including due diligence, term sheets, farm-in agreements, government consent processes and post-completion regulatory compliance. We handle both buy-side and sell-side mandates.

05

Gas Supply & Processing Agreements

We advise on gas commercialisation — including gas supply agreements, gas processing agreements, gas transportation arrangements and gas-to-power projects. Nigeria's gas monetisation agenda creates significant commercial and legal complexity; we help clients navigate it.

06

FPSO & Offshore Contracts

We advise on FPSO charter arrangements, offshore drilling contracts, offshore construction contracts and the complex contractual arrangements that underpin offshore oil and gas operations — including interface management, liability allocation and indemnity structures.

07

Renewables & Energy Transition

We advise on the growing renewables sector in Nigeria — including solar, wind and hybrid energy projects, off-grid electrification, power purchase agreements for renewable generation and the regulatory framework for independent power producers and mini-grid operators.

08

Joint Operating Agreements (JOAs)

We advise on the negotiation, drafting and amendment of joint operating agreements — including default and non-consent provisions, operatorship disputes, pre-emption rights and the interaction between JOA terms and Nigerian regulatory requirements.
