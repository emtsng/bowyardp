Abdulaziz Pitiko is an Associate at Bowyard Partners, working within the firm's Business Advisory and Infrastructure practices.

He advises clients on commercial strategy, project structuring, public-private partnership arrangements and infrastructure transactions — supporting the firm's role as a strategic adviser to clients building and financing large-scale projects in Nigeria's priority infrastructure sectors.

Abdulaziz brings analytical rigour and practical business sense to every engagement, with a commitment to advice that is commercially grounded and focused on enabling clients to build and launch with confidence.
